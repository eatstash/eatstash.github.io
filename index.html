Personalized meal planning

Meal planning is easy and fast. You can pick ingredients, like chicken or legumes, and let EatStash generate a meal plan for you. Or you can filter and browse your recipes to pick what you like.

Upload recipes from anywhere

Instantly add recipes from food blogs, YouTube, Instagram, Reddit, TikTok and even cookbooks. Upload with a url, a screenshot or from your camera, and we'll pull in the ingredients and directions for you.

Aisle-divided grocery lists

We even make shopping easy. With just two taps, you can turn your entire meal plan into a shareable grocery list, conveniently categorized by aisle.

Hands-free cooking mode

​Messy hands? That’s okay. Using our unique palm-to-camera feature, you can hover your hand over the camera to switch between ingredients and directions without touching your phone.

Search, sort, and filter

Have a specific recipe in mind? You can search by ingredient, sort by your personal recipe rating, or even by season. Your recipes are now always accessible, and you can filter through them like never before.

Automatic organization

EatStash automatically tags your recipes with useful categories for meal planning (examples include, "Vegetarian", "One-dish", "Quick", and "Instant Pot"). No need to manually organize your collection!

Home cooking the easy way

EatStash helps you enjoy home-cooked meals without the hassle of planning and organizing. Eat better, enhance your cooking skills, and save time and money. Make the most of those recipes you've been collecting for years!
