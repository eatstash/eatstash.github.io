Your entire recipe collection always at your fingertips

Say goodbye to scattered recipes and hello to the magic of Eatstash. Whether it's a Chrome bookmarks folder, a printed cookbook, an Instagram video, your mom's text, or a PDF, Eatstash leverages recent AI breakthroughs to bring all your recipes together in one place for the very first time!

Super simple meal planning

We've designed many user-friendly ways to put together a meal plan. You can make a pile of potential options as you browse your own recipes, or you can efficiently reuse a previous meal plan. If you're feeling decision fatigue, use Eatstash's AI-powered meal planning feature to take stress out of deciding what to cook. You tell it that you're feeling, say, something cozy that you've made before, and let it search your collection for you!

Hands-free cook mode

You shouldn't have to be scrolling so much. It disrupts that magical cooking flow and probably ruins your phone. We leverage good design and AI to give you a hands-free, voice-free way to navigate between steps in a recipe. No need to pause that podcast!

Search, sort and filter like you never have before

Find what you're looking for much faster. Filter your recipes by what's in season around you, or on sale at the local grocery store. Then sort by your personal rating, and search for recipes that can use up that leftover celery hanging out in your fridge. The possibilities are endless.

Easy grocery list creation

Turn your meal plan into a shareable grocery list with two taps.
