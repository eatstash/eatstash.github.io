Meal planning in seconds

Meal planning is easy and fast. You can specify the ingredients you’d like to cook with, like chicken or fish, or browse your recipes to pick exactly what you'd like.

Upload recipes from anywhere

Add recipes from social media, food blogs and even from cookbooks. Upload with a url, a screenshot or from your camera.

Aisle-divided grocery lists

We even make shopping easy. With just two taps, generate a grocery list categorized by aisle.

Hands-free cooking mode

​Messy hands? That’s okay. Using our unique palm-to-camera feature, you can hover your hand over the camera to switch between ingredients and directions without touching your phone.

Search, sort, and filter

Have a specific recipe in mind? You can search by ingredient, sort by your personal recipe rating, or even by season. Your recipes are now always accessible.

Automatic organization

EatStash automatically tags your recipes with useful categories (examples include, "Poultry,” "One-dish,” "Quick,” and "Instant Pot"). No need to manually organize your collection!
